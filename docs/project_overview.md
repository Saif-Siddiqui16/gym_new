# Project Overview: Gym CRM (SaaS)

## Purpose
The Gym CRM is a comprehensive, multi-tenant Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform designed to manage gym operations, memberships, staff, and financial activities. It provides tailored dashboards for various stakeholders to ensure efficient management and an enhanced member experience.

## Target Roles
- **Superadmin**: Global controller managing multiple gyms, SaaS plans, and system-wide audits.
- **Admin (Branch Admin)**: Oversees specific gym branches, manages branch-level settings, and reports.
- **Manager**: Handles day-to-day operations, member relations, and staff coordination.
- **Staff**: Manages front-desk tasks, attendance check-ins, and locker assignments.
- **Trainer**: Manages member assignments, workout/diet plans, and availability.
- **Member**: Accesses personal performance, membership details, class bookings, and shop.

## Core Modules
1.  **Dashboard**: Role-specific data visualization and metrics.
2.  **Membership Management**: Lifecycle management from inquiry to renewal/freeze.
3.  **Classes & Bookings**: Schedule management and session enrollment.
4.  **CRM & Sales**: Lead tracking, follow-ups, and conversion pipelines.
5.  **Finance & POS**: invoicing, expense tracking, and Point of Sale functionality.
6.  **Operations**: Inventory, equipment maintenance, and facility management.
7.  **HR & Payroll**: Staff management, scheduling, and automated payroll generation.
8.  **Shared Resources**: Diet plans, workout plans, and progress tracking.
